Our services:
Clinic36 offers a number of services - the dispensing of both methadone, suboxone, and subutex, all depending on which treatment options clients might choose, in conjunction with the opinion of a doctor assigned to them. The clinic ensures patients have frequent checkups with an in-house medical doctor, under the supervision of whom, clients of the clinic can choose to undergo titration (the raising or lowering of the dose according to the needs of the patient), maintenence, or a medically supervised detox, where a patient chooses to slowly taper themselves off of the medication they're currently on. Clinic 36 is also a liver clinic; a clinic at which patients with Hepatitis, or other liver diseases or complications can seek medical advice, and increasingly effective treatment. New treatment options for hepatitis A and B have recently become available; depending on a variety of factors, hepatitis A and B are actually curable.
